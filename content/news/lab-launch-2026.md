Finding Luca is now live.

This is the official launch of our research laboratory. We're starting small — a focused team, a clear set of questions, and a long-term commitment to pursuing answers that matter.

## What we're focused on

Our initial research areas are:

- **Precision Diagnostics** — catching disease earlier, more accurately
- **Regenerative Medicine** — understanding and amplifying the body's ability to heal itself
- **AI-Driven Drug Discovery** — using machine learning to compress discovery timelines

We'll be publishing updates, papers, and essays regularly on this site.

## Get involved

If you're a researcher, student, or just someone deeply interested in the future of medicine — we want to hear from you. Use the contact page to get in touch.

This is only the beginning.
